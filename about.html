About LiF

Rooted in love, built through co-creation.

This page gives visitors the story, values, principles, and purpose behind Love is Foundation. Basically the part where the organization stops being a logo and starts being understandable.

Our story

A foundation for connection, dignity, and collective growth.

Love is Foundation brings people together to explore practical ways of supporting community wellbeing. The site should communicate the story clearly, while still leaving space for the organization to keep evolving.

  • Values-led community building
  • Trust, transparency, and accountability
  • Shared learning and co-creation
  • Support for resilient communities
