Community platform

Move from visiting to participating.

This page explains the engagement platform, whether the final choice becomes Hylo, Sutra, or something else. The website should not pretend to be the whole community. That would be a very website thing to do.

Flow

The public site should guide people into active community.

Keep the website informational and polished. Use the platform for discussions, groups, events, and ongoing participation.

1Discover LiFHomepage, social, search, referral
2Choose a pathwayLearn, event, volunteer, resource
3Join platformHylo / Sutra / chosen platform
4ParticipateProjects, discussions, circles, events

Platform features

What the platform should support.

Groups & spaces

Separate areas for projects, youth engagement, volunteers, and community circles.

Discussions

Structured dialogue and announcements without losing everything in one endless chat.

Events

RSVPs, reminders, calendar links, and meeting follow-ups.

Resources

Shared documents, links, guides, and learning materials.

Member directory

Help people find each other by interest, role, or project area.

Onboarding

Clear first steps so new members know what to do after joining.
